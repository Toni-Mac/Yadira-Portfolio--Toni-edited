Branding

I created my logo to represent my personality and strengths as a diverse and well-rounded designer. I wanted to incorporate my initials into the logo using sharp edges to illustrate my design style and quality and gradients to portray my diversity as a designer.

Color Palette

To have a personal identity and style as a designer is extremely important in the Web/Graphic Design industry. In deciding on the colors for my branding, I wanted colors that would portray my personality and the diversity that I embrace as both an individual and a professional. I used different shades of yellow, blue and pink, all very vibrant colors that compliment each other to form the perfect color palette.
