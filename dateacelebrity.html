Date A Celebrity

VANCOUVER, BRITISH COLUMBIA. 2018. ROLE: SOLE DESIGNER.

Date A Celebrity is a matching game based on the idea of finding a love match with a celebrity. The purpose of the game is for the player to play until all matches are found and the last match that is found is the celebrity that he or she would win a date with. If the player loses, the player has to start over or go back to the drawing board, Tinder.

Branding

The branding is aimed towards singles between the ages of 18 to 50, both male and female. I used handwritten typography for the logo as well as the website to give a romantic, sensual feeling to the website and also imagery that portrayed sensual, sexy, and romantic for its users.

Color Palette

I used bright, vibrant, romantic colors for all of the elements on the website to symbolize love and romanticism, and the famous love and Valentine's Day character, Cupid, for singles looking to find love.

Planning and Development

For planning and development, I was able to better code my website by first creating wireframes that enabled me to style my stylesheet exactly how I planned for it to be styled. The purpose of this project was to create this site using Javascript and CSS. However, I created all of my images using Photoshop and Illustrator to portray the look and feel I wanted to go for in making it as romantic as possible.
