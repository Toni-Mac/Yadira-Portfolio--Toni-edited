About Me

Hi! I'm Yadira (pronounced Jahdira), a Bahamian-born, skilled, sociable, and enthusiastic Graphic Designer and Web Developer based in Surrey, BC. I am a recent graduate of the BCIT Technical Web Development program in Vancouver, BC, and I have a strong background in Graphic Design (B.A in Communication Design 2015), Design Research, producing Print and Digital collateral, Social Media Marketing, SEO Optimization, and User Testing that allows me to better understand ideal user experiences for your business. I'm extremely passionate about good design and delivering powerful design solutions that challenges the market and I am always open to learning and experimenting with new design trends. My attention for detail and creative eye is key to creating beautiful, user-friendly websites and designs that not only appeals, but impacts.

I love the outdoors and nature, travelling, food and culture, and when I'm not working, I'm either watching my favourite chick flicks, learning new design skills, or spending time with family and friends.
